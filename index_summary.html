Proposed Fast PDR Flow

Measures used

Best model

is defined based on the waveform misfit. Best model has the smallest waveform misfit

Error

measures the Least Square misfit between vertical surface displacement for input and best model (e.g. Pietrzak, 2007; Gusman, 2019; Ulrich, 2019; Ho, 2021) (based on potential tsunami energy)

\(E_{pot, tsu} = \oint \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot g \cdot \rho \cdot h^2\)

âžž \(\sqrt{\sum{(\Delta u_{init} - \Delta u_{mod})^2}}\)
